Intelligence Built into Every Camera

Edge AI Processing

Detect, Don't Just Record

Every camera runs a built-in Neural Processing Unit that processes video at the edge — no cloud round-trip, no bandwidth strain.

Detect and classify people and vehicles in real time. Set virtual tripwires for cross-line counting. Monitor restricted zones for unauthorized entry. All analytics happen on-device — alerts arrive in milliseconds, not minutes.

Facial Recognition & LPR

Know Who's There — Instantly

The ECC500 flagship camera takes AI further with on-device facial recognition and license plate reading.

Flag specific individuals for instant alerts. Track visitor timelines with face-to-face search. Read and log license plates at entrances and loading docks — searchable across your entire network.

Identification happens at the edge, keeping sensitive biometric data off the cloud.

Custom Alert Rules

Your Rules. Instant Alerts.

Define exactly what matters — and silence everything else.

Set up restricted area detection for after-hours zones. Create cross-line alerts at entry points. Trigger loitering notifications when someone lingers too long. Get tampering alerts if a camera is blocked or moved.

Route alerts by priority — push critical events to your phone, send routine logs to email, or forward to your SIEM via webhook.

Built-in Edge Storage

No NVR. No Single Point of Failure.

Each camera stores footage locally on up to 512 GB of built-in solid-state storage — no external hard drives, no server racks.

If your network drops, recording continues. When connectivity returns, everything syncs to the cloud.

With IP67 weatherproofing, IK10 vandal resistance, and operation down to −40°C, these cameras survive where it matters most.

AI-Powered Search

Powered by generative AI, the search engine understands context, not just keywords. It works in any language, across all cameras.
