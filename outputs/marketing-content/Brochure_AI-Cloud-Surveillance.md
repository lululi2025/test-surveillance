## PAGE 1 — SOLUTION OVERVIEW

### See More. Store More. Manage Less.

Traditional surveillance demands dedicated NVR hardware, complex wiring, and on-site maintenance at every location. For multi-site businesses, the cost and complexity multiply fast.

EnGenius AI Cloud Surveillance takes a different approach. Every camera stores footage on-device, processes AI analytics at the edge, and reports to a single cloud dashboard. Deploy a camera, plug in PoE, and start monitoring — from anywhere.

**Three pillars that set this system apart:**

| Pillar | What It Means |
|--------|---------------|
| **Edge AI, Built In** | Each camera runs an AI NPU on-device — detecting people, vehicles, and events in real time without sending raw video to the cloud. |
| **On-Camera Storage, No NVR** | Up to 512GB of encrypted storage per camera. Record 24/7 for up to 30 days without external hardware. |
| **One Cloud, Every Camera** | Deploy, configure, monitor, and search — all from the EnGenius Cloud dashboard or the Cloud To-Go mobile app. |

**Key Architecture Benefits:**

- **Zero-touch provisioning** — Cameras auto-register to your cloud organization. No manual IP configuration, no DVR/NVR setup.
- **Edge processing** — AI analytics run on the camera's NPU, not in the cloud. This means lower bandwidth, faster alerts, and no subscription for AI features.
- **Encrypted local storage** — Footage stays on the camera with AES-256 encryption. No third-party cloud dependency for core recording.
- **Cloud + NVS redundancy** — Need extra protection? Add an EnGenius NVS for centralized backup, or use cloud storage for offsite redundancy.

## PAGE 3 — AI ANALYTICS CAPABILITIES

### AI That Detects, Not Just Records

Every EnGenius AI Camera includes a built-in Neural Processing Unit (NPU) that runs inference models directly on the device. No cloud round-trip, no extra licensing.

| AI Feature | What It Does | Available On |
|------------|-------------|--------------|
| **People Detection & Tracking** | Detects and counts people in frame. Search history by person. | ECC100, ECC120, ECC500 |
| **Vehicle Detection & Tracking** | Detects and counts vehicles. Search history by vehicle. | ECC100, ECC120, ECC500 |
| **Restricted Area Detection** | Triggers alerts when objects enter a defined zone. | ECC100, ECC120, ECC500 |
