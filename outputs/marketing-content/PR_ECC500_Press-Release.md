## EnGenius Introduces the ECC500: A Cloud-Managed 4K AI Outdoor Dome Camera with 512GB Built-In Storage

*The Cam8MP Dome IP67 delivers edge AI analytics, 4K clarity, and NVR-free recording — purpose-built for SMBs managing multi-site security from the cloud.*

---

**Costa Mesa, CA — [Date TBD]** — EnGenius Technologies, a global provider of cloud-managed networking and smart security solutions, today announced the **ECC500 (Cam8MP Dome IP67)**, the latest addition to its AI Cloud Camera lineup. Designed for small and medium businesses that need enterprise-grade surveillance without the complexity, the ECC500 combines 4K image quality, built-in AI analytics, and 512GB of onboard encrypted storage — all managed through the EnGenius Cloud platform.

### The Challenge: SMB Security Shouldn't Require an NVR Rack

Traditional surveillance systems force businesses to invest in separate NVR hardware, manage local storage infrastructure, and rely on manual footage review to find critical events. For multi-site operators, IT teams, and managed service providers, this approach doesn't scale.

### The Solution: AI at the Edge, Storage on the Camera, Management in the Cloud

The ECC500 eliminates the need for dedicated NVR hardware by embedding 512GB of storage directly in the camera, supporting up to 30 days of continuous 24/7 recording with automatic recycling. All recordings are protected with AES-256 encryption, ensuring footage remains secure and unplayable on unauthorized platforms.

Powered by a built-in AI NPU, the ECC500 processes video at the edge — enabling real-time facial recognition, license plate recognition (LPR), people and vehicle detection, restricted area monitoring, and cross-line detection. AI-powered search reduces footage review time from hours to minutes, letting operators find specific people, vehicles, or events instantly.

### Cloud-Managed from Anywhere

The ECC500 integrates seamlessly with the EnGenius Cloud platform, enabling IT administrators and MSPs to deploy, configure, and monitor cameras across multiple sites from a single dashboard — no on-site visits required. The Cloud To-Go mobile app extends monitoring to iOS and Android devices, providing real-time alerts and AI-powered event search on the go.

### Built for Real-World Conditions

With an IP67-rated enclosure, IK10 impact resistance, and an operating temperature range of -40°C to 60°C, the ECC500 is engineered for demanding outdoor environments — from parking lots and building perimeters to loading docks and campuses. The aluminum alloy and polycarbonate housing withstands weather extremes and vandalism attempts.

### Ideal Use Cases

- **Retail:** Monitor storefronts and parking areas with AI-powered people and vehicle tracking. Detect loitering or unauthorized access in restricted zones.
- **Hospitality:** Secure hotel perimeters, entrances, and parking facilities with 4K clarity and real-time alerts.
- **Education:** Protect campus grounds with facial recognition and cross-line detection for controlled entry points.
- **Warehousing & Logistics:** Track vehicle movement at loading docks and detect intrusion in restricted areas.
- **Multi-site operations:** MSPs and IT teams manage all locations from one cloud dashboard without truck rolls.
